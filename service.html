HOW DOES IT WORK

One of the special benefits Travel Blue offers is a FREE Lost & Found Service.

Our products are labelled with a special FLF ID number. After registering this number, your product will be matched with your details on our website.

In case a registered product has been lost and found, we will immediately contact you with full details on how to retrieve your lost property
